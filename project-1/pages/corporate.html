About our "Non-Retro" Typewriters

Founded in 1945, "Non-Retro" Typewriter has been delivering high-quality, typewriters to enthusiasts worldwide. Our mission is to preserve the elegance and craftsmanship of writing machines to help build a better future, one letter at a time.

Located in Barrie, CA, we pride ourselves on combining top-of-the-line design with modern reliability.
