About Our Typewriter

Our "Non-Retro" Typewriter is crafted from durable materials and designed that are ergonomically spaced to prevent what we like to call "Sticky Keys".

  • Ergonomic mechanical keys with satisfying tactile feedback and response times
  • Elegant black-and-chrome design
  • Lightweight and portable for home or office
  • Compatible with standard typewriter ribbons
